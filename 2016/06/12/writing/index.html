发表于 2016-06-12
writing
  • As is shown vividly above
    正如上图生动展示的那样
  • In crucial periods of life
    在人生关键阶段
  • Cannot be given too much attention or consideration
    给与再多关注和考虑也不为过
  • On the contrary ,
  • Only by doing so can we …
    只有这样做我们才能…
  • This is to say ,
    也就是说
  • Stephen Coney once said that there are three constants in life :changes,choice and principles
    生命中有三种东西是不变的：变化、选择和原则
  • To do this or to do that is a question
    做这个还是做那个，这是个问题
  • …are no exception
    …也不例外
  • The pessimistic people only see despair,while the optimistic people see hope in it
    悲观的人只看到失望，乐观的人看到希望
  • Laugh till the end
    笑到最后
  • Thought-provoking
    发人深省的
  • It is advisable for us to …
    …是明智之举
  • …goes far beyond doubt
    …是毋庸置疑的
  • Up until a few decades ago, … . Now …
    直到几十年前… ;现在…
  • Take a broader look at …
    把视野放宽点看…
  • As so often ,
    正如很多时候
  • To be sure ，
    诚然，
  • A is to B what C is to D
    A之于B正如C之于D
  • The more … ,the more …
    The life of a man is the process of trying. The more one tries, the better his life will be.
    人的一生就是进行尝试，尝试得越多，生活就越美好
    --Emerson （爱默生）
  • The subject of life is making choices.
    人生的课题就是选择
    --Sartre ( 萨特 )
